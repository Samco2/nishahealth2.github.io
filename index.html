WHAT WE DO

CONNECTING HEALTHCARE

Oncology, mental health patients and many others struggle with multiple providers acting disjointly and independently when nobody eventually cares about the long-term outcome only doing their narrow part of the treatment. Our mission is to transform this into a controlled journey with data collected and analyzed by a proprietary Al algorithm to give financial and health analysis of the treatment progress and facilitate next steps.

YOUR HEALTH DATA

Nisha Health Al is responsible for collecting all of your healthcare data on your behalf from many organizations storing it today: your health insurance, hospitals, labs, imaging centers, etc. You tell us who you've been dealing with and we will go and fetch it for you. This is done through a modern technology and innovations.

ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

How much will I have to pay out of the pocket if I go with chemotherapy vs precision medicine assuming both are multi-step processes spread out in time and given my current clinical situation? These and other questions are very difficult to predict for a human but can be easily modelled by Al based on your insurance benefits, deductibles, out of pocket limits, various costs of treatment and timing.
